# Adding camelAI to Claude

Integrate camelAI directly into Claude to access powerful data analysis capabilities within your AI conversations. Query your databases, generate insights, and get SQL results all through Claude's interface.

<Note>
For the latest official guidance from Anthropic, see their support article on remote MCP integrations: [Custom integrations using remote MCP](https://support.anthropic.com/en/articles/11175166-about-custom-integrations-using-remote-mcp#h_3d1a65aded).
</Note>

## Prerequisites

Before setting up the integration, ensure you have:
<Warning>
Custom integrations using remote MCP are available for Claude Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans. This feature is currently in beta.
</Warning>

## Adding the Integration

The process varies slightly depending on your Claude plan type:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Claude Pro/Max">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
        Go to Settings > Integrations in your Claude interface
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Custom Integration">
        Locate the "Integrations" section and click "Add custom integration" at the bottom
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Server URL">
        Add your camelAI remote MCP server URL: `https://app.camelai.com/mcp`
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete Setup">
        Click "Add" to finish configuring your integration
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Claude Team/Enterprise">
    <Note>
    Only Primary Owners or Owners can enable integrations on Team and Enterprise accounts. Once configured, individual users can authenticate with their own credentials.
    </Note>
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
        Go to Settings > Integrations in your Claude interface
      </Step>
      <Step title="Switch to Organization">
        Toggle to "Organization integrations" at the top of the page
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Custom Integration">
        Locate the "Integrations" section and click "Add custom integration" at the bottom
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Integration Name">
        Enter "camelAI" for the Integration Name
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Server URL">
        Add your camelAI remote MCP server URL: `https://app.camelai.com/mcp`
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete Setup">
        Click "Add" to finish configuring your integration
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Authentication

After adding the integration, you'll need to authenticate:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to camelAI">
    Find camelAI in your integrations and click "Connect"
  </Step>
  <Step title="OAuth Authentication">
    You'll be redirected to camelAI's authentication page to sign in with your camelAI account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant Permissions">
    Review and approve the requested permissions for Claude to access your data sources
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using camelAI with Claude

Once connected, camelAI tools will be available in your Claude conversations.

## Managing the Integration

### Editing or Removing

To modify your camelAI integration:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Go to Settings > Integrations in Claude
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find camelAI">
    Locate camelAI in the "Integrations" section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Modify Integration">
    Click "Remove" or select the three dots to edit configuration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow Prompts">
    Complete the removal or editing process as prompted
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Disconnecting Authentication

You can disconnect your camelAI authentication at any time:

- Through Claude: Settings > Integrations > Disconnect camelAI

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Integration Not Appearing">
    **Symptoms:** camelAI doesn't show up in your integrations list

    **Solutions:**
    - Verify you have a Claude Pro, Max, Team, or Enterprise subscription
    - Check that you correctly entered the server URL: `https://app.camelai.com/mcp`
    - Try removing and re-adding the integration
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Authentication Failing">
    **Symptoms:** Cannot connect to camelAI during OAuth flow

    **Solutions:**
    - Ensure you have a valid camelAI account
    - Try disconnecting and reconnecting the integration
    - Verify your camelAI account has active database connections
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Tools Not Working">
    **Symptoms:** camelAI tools fail when Claude tries to use them

    **Solutions:**
    - Verify your database connections are active in camelAI
    - Check that you've enabled the tools in the "Search and tools" menu
    - Ensure your camelAI account has access to the data sources you're querying
    - Try disconnecting and reconnecting the OAuth integration
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
