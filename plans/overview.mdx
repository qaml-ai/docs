camelAI is free to start. Sign up and you can chat with the agent right away on **Camel Free**, our hosted model that costs nothing to use. No credit card required.

Paid plans raise the platform limits (deployed apps, automations, storage, seats) and include monthly credits for premium models like Claude and GPT. You can also unlock premium models without subscribing at all; see [Unlocking premium models](#unlocking-premium-models) below.

## Unlocking premium models

Every plan includes Camel Free, and it handles everyday tasks well. It runs on shared capacity, so it can slow down at busy times. Paid plans get priority over free traffic.

Premium models (Claude, GPT, Gemini, and more) cost money to run. camelAI passes that cost through at the provider's rate with no markup. There are four ways to cover it, and all four work on any plan, including Free:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Subscribe to a plan">
    Starter, Pro, and Team come with monthly credits matching what you pay: $10, $40, and $50 per seat. You get fresh credits every month, and they cover every model camelAI hosts, so you can switch freely between Claude, GPT, Gemini, and the rest. Subscribing also raises your platform limits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Top up credits">
    Buy a prepaid credit pack any time from **Settings → Usage**, with or without a subscription. 1 credit = $1, usage is billed at the provider's cost, and top-up credits never expire. This is the way to use premium models on the Free plan without subscribing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bring your own API key">
    Add a key from Anthropic, OpenAI, OpenRouter, AWS Bedrock, or a custom endpoint under **Settings → AI Provider**. Your provider bills you directly, and your model list comes from whatever that provider serves. See [Model Providers](/plans/model-providers) for which provider unlocks which models.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sign in with OpenAI">
    Already pay for ChatGPT? Connect your OpenAI account with a short sign-in flow (no API key to copy) and GPT models run on the allowance included in your ChatGPT plan, at no extra cost. If you have a ChatGPT subscription, this is by far the cheapest way to use GPT models on camelAI.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### How these fit together

- Plan credits and top-up credits share one balance, and usage draws from it no matter where the credits came from.
- An API key replaces camelAI's hosted models entirely, including Camel Free. All requests go through your one chosen provider. If you want broad model coverage on a single key, use [OpenRouter](/plans/model-providers).
- Sign in with OpenAI is the exception: it works alongside any other setup and only takes over GPT models.

### If you run out of credits

Your chat keeps working. You can continue on Camel Free, and your apps, automations, and workspace are untouched. To get back on a premium model, top up, upgrade your plan, add an API key, or sign in with OpenAI. Subscribers get fresh credits at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Topping up credits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open usage settings">
    Open **Settings → Usage** in your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Top up credits">
    Click **Top up credits.**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check out">
    Pick a credit pack and check out through Stripe.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Available pack sizes are shown in the dialog.

## Why prepaid

Credits are paid for up front, the same way OpenAI, Anthropic, and OpenRouter sell them. You only spend what you put in, and we only run requests against funded accounts, which keeps fraud and runaway bills off the table for both sides. The monthly platform fee is billed normally on a recurring basis through Stripe.

## Changing plans

Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from **Settings → Billing.** Plan changes go through the Stripe billing portal.
